Delete Your Account

You may request to delete your Across Time account and associated data in either of the following ways:

Option 1: Email

Send a request to acrosstimemessages@gmail.com. We will process your request and delete your account and associated data.

Option 2: In the App

Open the Across Time app, go to Settings (or your profile), and tap Delete Account. This will remove your account and associated data.
