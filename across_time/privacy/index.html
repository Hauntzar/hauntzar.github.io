Privacy Policy

Across Time — Last updated: February 2025

Across Time ("we", "our", or "the app") collects and uses your data to provide the time capsule messaging service. This policy describes what we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Account: Email address and sign-in provider (Google, Apple, Facebook) for authentication.
  • Messages: Text and media you add, delivery dates, and recipient contacts.
  • Contacts: Names, email addresses, and phone numbers you add or import from your device.
  • Usage: Data needed to deliver your messages on the chosen future dates.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to operate the time capsule service: storing your messages, scheduling delivery, and sending them to your chosen recipients on the specified dates. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Storage and Security

Your content is stored securely on our infrastructure (AWS). We use industry-standard practices to protect your data.

Third-Party Services

We use sign-in providers (Google, Apple, Facebook) for authentication and payment processors (Stripe, Apple IAP, Google Play Billing) for purchases. Their privacy policies apply to those interactions.

Your Rights

You can delete your account and data through the app or by contacting us. If you are in the European Economic Area or other regions with data protection laws, you may have additional rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability).

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us through the app or at the support email listed in the app stores.
